Who We Are

Signals from the Grassroots is a youth initiative that brings young people together to share knowledge and opinions about SDGs through meaningful discussions. We aim to build a culture of dialogue among young people as a way of stimulating them to take actions to solve the world's pressing problems.
Our strong belief in youth as the cornerstone for sustainable positive change inspired us to join global efforts to advocate for youths' active involvement in decision-making processes in society.
However, we cannot advocate for youth participation in decision-making processes if youth are not well-equipped with decision-making skills. Therefore, we aim to equip youth with these skills through building a culture of dialogue amongst them.

We also bring community engagement opportunities closer to youth as facilitation for them to put to action what they learn, like volunteering, internship and job opportunities. Most importantly, we encourage and challenge youth to establish initiatives to tackle their society's problems.

SftG members are called SIGNALS because they are constantly illuminating positive change in their respective countries and regions.
With Signals from 50+ nations and 5 continents, SftG is a worldwide movement of change that benefits not only the youth but all members of society.

Our Vision

To be the hub of globally enlightened and dignified young men and women championing positive change

What We Do

Have you ever noticed how dialogue is the very heart of shaping our society and the world?
From a nation's parliament to the United Nations’ General Assembly, dialogue defines all!

With realization of its immense power in defining society, SftG aims to build a culture of dialogue among tomorrow’s leaders. We weave together conversation, respect, creativity, critical thinking and analysis to create a space for exchanging wisdom, imagining new possibilities and practicing community leadership.

SftG connects youth across their differences to take action about the things that matter by:-

  1. I. Providing a safe platform of learning and inspiring one another
  2. II. Availing skill-development and community-engagement opportunities that enable youth to take action to uplift themselves and their communities

We integrate a variety of constantly evolving youth engagement strategies throughout our programming and operations to foster youth development. In all our programs, we work directly with young people to elevate their stories.

We create channels—both online and in person—for young people to drive conversations. Youth voices are front and center in our conferences, workshops, videos, webinars and blog posts.

Signals from the grassroots also advocates for active youth engagement in community affairs. We advocate for meaningful youth engagement where youth are viewed as equal partners with adults in decision-making processes. We strive to change the notorious phrase of “for the youth” to “with the youth.” We strongly believe that youth need to actively engage in societal activities so as to learn through the process, rather than being marginalized as on-lookers as adults engross all the opportunities.
In our capacity strengthening of community systems, we highlight young people’s assets and emphasize the creation of safe spaces where youth can learn from each other while developing healthy relationships with supportive adults.
